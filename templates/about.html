About the SMS AI Assistant


Improbability Labs Inc. proudly presents the SMS AI Assistant, a revolutionary product that transforms everyday interactions into personalized experiences. This assistant offers a unique blend of advanced AI technologies, including Llama 3 from Groqcloud and OpenAI's ChatGPT, coupled with a robust MySQL backend. It leverages state-of-the-art algorithms to provide seamless interactions via SMS, tailored specifically to your preferences.

Experience customization like never before with options to personalize your AI assistant's name, origin, gender, personality type, and response style. These tailored settings ensure that every interaction is uniquely suited to your style and preferences, making your AI companion truly one-of-a-kind.

For just $15 a month, users gain access to dedicated SMS numbers that align with their local area code, ensuring a smooth and familiar communication process. Our AI assistant does more than just answer questions; it engages with you, enhances your daily routines, and keeps you informed by conducting sophisticated Google searches to provide the most relevant information in real-time.

With the SMS AI Assistant from Improbability Labs, you're not just using a service; you're engaging with the future of personalized communication. Subscribe today and start experiencing the next level of interactive AI.

About Improbability Labs


Improbability Labs Inc. is a technology firm led by Rahim Khoja, our CEO and sole developer. We specialize in using High-Performance Computing (HPC) to tackle intricate challenges across various sectors. Our focus is on research and development, aiming to create practical, efficient solutions by harnessing the capabilities of HPC and artificial intelligence.

Our approach is rooted in pragmatism, dedicated to crafting technologies that are not only innovative but directly address the real-world issues we face today. We extensively utilize Linux environments and data management tools like SLURM to build and manage our projects, ensuring robust and effective results.

At Improbability Labs, we believe in the power of technology to make a significant impact, and we strive to demonstrate this through every project we undertake.
